News & Updates

What is happening at AURA Lab.

A running record of new publications, fellowships, appointments, talks, and milestones from the group. Press and collaboration enquiries are welcome.

Sep 2025

AURA Lab opens at KL University (Deemed to be University)

Dr. Bahadur Sk joins the Department of Chemistry as Assistant Professor and establishes AURA Lab (Advanced Unimolecular Radiative Afterglow), beginning recruitment for PhD and project students.

Apr 2025

New paper in J. Phys. Chem. Lett.

Synchronizing the nπ* Transition Dipole in a Benzoyl-Linked N-Fused Ring for Highly Efficient Yellow Afterglow appears in J. Phys. Chem. Lett. — first authorship from the AURA Lab principal investigator.

2025

Adv. Mater. publication on through-bond interactions

Collaborative work on selective lower-occupied through-bond interactions for efficient organic phosphorescence enabling high-resolution long-wavelength afterglow is published in Advanced Materials.

2025

J. Mater. Chem. C study on TADF emitter optimisation

New paper on donor linkage and acceptor nitrogen-atom position optimisation in TADF emitters, improving external quantum efficiency and efficiency roll-off in OLEDs.

Feb 2025

Return to India as Research Associate at IACS

Began a Research Associate position at the Indian Association for the Cultivation of Science (Kolkata), working on computational materials design for organic semiconductor devices.

Apr 2024

UEC Postdoc Fellowship

Selected as a UEC Postdoc fellow at the University of Electro-Communications (Tokyo), continuing research on organic afterglow materials, devices, and high-resolution afterglow imaging.

Oct 2023

Adv. Sci. paper on triplet-singlet FRET for bright red afterglow

New work on selective triplet–singlet Förster-resonance energy transfer for bright red afterglow emission appears in Advanced Functional Materials.

2023

Anti-counterfeit afterglow paper

Collaborative paper on continuous condensed triplet accumulation for irradiance-induced anti-counterfeit afterglow appears in Advanced Science.

2022

UEC Postdoctoral Fellowship begins

Began the UEC Postdoctoral Fellowship at the University of Electro-Communications, Tokyo — building expertise in afterglow imaging, organic materials, and device fabrication.

Dec 2020

PhD awarded by IISER Bhopal

Completed PhD on multichromophoric organic systems, solid-state emission, and long-lived emissive materials at IISER Bhopal.
