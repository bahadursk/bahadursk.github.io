Group

The AURA Lab research group.

AURA Lab is being built from the ground up at KL University (Deemed to be University). The principal investigator is currently recruiting motivated PhD, MSc, and project students alongside postdoctoral collaborators. Existing collaborations span India, Japan, and beyond.

The group emphasises rigorous synthesis, careful spectroscopy, mechanistic thinking, and clear scientific writing — with strong publication and presentation outcomes.

Members are encouraged to present at national and international meetings and to publish in leading photonics and materials chemistry journals.
