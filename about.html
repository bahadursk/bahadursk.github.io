About Me

Dr. Bahadur Sk

I am an experienced researcher with expertise in organic chemistry, spectroscopy, organic electronics, and materials chemistry. I currently serve as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Chemistry at KL Deemed to be University.

Previously, I worked as a Postdoctoral Researcher at the University of Electro-Communications (Tokyo) and as a C. V. Raman Fellow at the Indian Institute of Science (IISc) in Bengaluru. I earned my Ph.D. in Chemistry from the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research (IISER) Bhopal.
