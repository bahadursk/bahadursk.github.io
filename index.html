About Me

I am a researcher working at the intersection of spectroscopy, photonics, and materials science. My group, the AURA Lab, focuses on developing energy-efficient, lightweight, flexible, and non-toxic organic emitters and understanding radiative decay processes for next-generation photonics applications such as lighting, display devices, security & storage devices. Previously, I was a postdoctoral fellow at the University of Electro-Communications, Tokyo and at the Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bangalore. :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}
