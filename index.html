Research Overview

Long-Lived Organic Emitters

RTP, TADF & Persistent Afterglow

Development of organic molecular and polymeric systems exhibiting room-temperature phosphorescence, thermally activated delayed fluorescence, and ultralong afterglow.

Excited-State Photophysics

Charge Transfer & Energy Relaxation

Understanding radiative and non-radiative decay processes using steady-state and time-resolved spectroscopic techniques.

Functional Organic Materials

Molecular Design for Photonics

Rational design of donor–acceptor systems for applications in OLEDs, sensing, security inks, and photonic devices.

Principal Investigator

Dr. Bahadur Sk

C. V. Raman Postdoctoral Fellow at the Materials Research Centre (MRC), Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bangalore.

My research focuses on the development of advanced organic emissive materials and understanding their excited-state dynamics across multiple time scales.

The AURA Lab aims to develop energy-efficient, lightweight, non-toxic photonic materials for applications in lighting, displays, sensing, and security technologies.
