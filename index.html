Assistant Professor

AURA Lab, KL Deemed to be University

My scientific curiosity began with a simple question: Why do different objects have different colors? This led me to explore excited-state processes, organic emitters, and long-lived luminescence.

At the AURA Lab, we focus on developing energy-efficient, lightweight, flexible, and non-toxic organic photonic materials for lighting, displays, and security applications.
