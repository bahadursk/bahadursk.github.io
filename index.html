I'm Bahadur Sk

I'm a Researcher at IISER Bhopal and Pursuing Research at the interface between Spectroscopy and Materials Science.
Let's start scrolling and learn more about me.

Bahadur Sk

Senior Research Fellow

https://orcid.org/0000-0002-7098-5429 ORCID

The highlights of my research work include (i) development of multifunctional bioprobe based on biologically important heterocycle pyrido[1,2-a]indole exhibiting strong fluorescence in solution as well as solid state, (ii) understanding the solid state fluorescence based on molecular interactions in crystal, (iii) synthesis of donor-acceptor (D-A) types intramolecular charge transfer (ICT) molecules and polymers, (iv) tuning the intramolecular charge transfer (ICT) in D-A materials leading to white light emission, (v) exploration of ICT at nanoscale level and (vi) understanding the photophysical and electrochemical properties of D-A materials. The extensive spectroscopic investigation coupled with computational analysis and microscopic studies both in vivo and in vitro have been carried out employing a range of new molecular materials.
I would like to pursue research on the development of novel electron donor-acceptor (D-A) based ICT materials (molecules and polymers) for both fundamental and application perspectives.
